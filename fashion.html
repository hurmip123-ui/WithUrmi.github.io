Fashion & Style

Curating mindful style, capsule wardrobes, and personal expression.

1. Building a Capsule Wardrobe

A capsule wardrobe focuses on high-quality, versatile items that can be easily mixed and matched to reduce clutter and morning stress.

  • The Essentials: Standard items like a well-fitted classic white button-down shirt, neutral trousers, and a structured blazer.
  • Color Palette: Stick to 3-4 cohesive colors (such as beige, black, cream, and olive) to ensure every piece goes well together.

2. Seasonal Trends

Incorporate seasonal pieces selectively into your wardrobe to complement your timeless foundational staples.

  • Textural Fabrics: Experiment with rich textures like linen for summer months, and heavy knitwear or wool mixes during cooler seasons.
  • Accessories: Use subtle gold jewelry, classic leather belts, and structured bags to transform simple casual basics into elegant statement looks.
