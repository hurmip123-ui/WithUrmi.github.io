Reversing PCOS & PCOD Naturally

A simple guide to healing through nutrition and lifestyle adjustments.

1. Core Nutritional Strategies

  • Low-Glycemic Index Foods: Prioritize whole grains like oats, brown rice, quinoa, and lentils to steady insulin levels.
  • Healthy Fats & Fiber: Incorporate walnuts, flaxseeds, chia seeds, and plenty of green leafy vegetables to support hormonal balance.
  • Anti-Inflammatory Additions: Use turmeric, ginger, and cinnamon daily to reduce internal metabolic stress.

2. Crucial Dietary Shifts

  • Eliminate Refined Sugars: Cut out white sugar, sodas, and processed bakery items to prevent insulin spikes.
  • Spearmint Tea: Drink 1-2 cups of spearmint tea daily, which helps naturally reduce excess male hormones (androgens).

3. Therapeutic Yoga Practices

  • Supta Baddha Konasana (Reclined Bound Angle Pose): Helps stimulate abdominal organs and improves blood flow to the pelvic region.
  • Bhujangasana (Cobra Pose): Tones the reproductive organs and reduces stress-induced cortisol.
